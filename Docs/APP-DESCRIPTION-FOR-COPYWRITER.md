## App Overview

AstroPowerMaps is a mobile-first astrology web application that combines two core features:

1. **Astrocartography Map** ‚Äî An interactive world map showing the user's 40 planetary lines, revealing which geographic locations on Earth are most aligned with their birth chart
2. **2026 Forecast** ‚Äî A personalized year-ahead calendar showing which months and days are best for different life goals (Love, Career, Growth, Home)

The app uses the user's exact birth data (date, time, location) to calculate:
- Their "Big Three" (Sun sign, Moon sign, Rising sign)
- Their planetary lines across the globe
- Their power months and best days throughout 2026
- AI-generated daily insights personalized to their chart

The primary value proposition presented in the app is: **"Two maps. One purchase. Your 2026 Forecast tells you WHEN. Your Birth Chart tells you WHERE. Together, they show you exactly how to plan your year."**

## How the Astrology Works (Technical)

This section explains how the app calculates its astrological data ‚Äî important for the copywriter to understand this is based on real astronomy, not invented.

### Planetary Position Calculations

The app uses the **VSOP87 theory** (Variations S√©culaires des Orbites Plan√©taires) for calculating planetary positions. This is the same mathematical model used by:
- NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory
- Major astronomical almanacs
- Professional astronomy software

**For the Moon:** Uses a full lunar theory algorithm that accounts for the Moon's complex orbital mechanics.

**For Pluto:** Uses simplified orbital elements (VSOP87 only covers planets through Neptune).

### Julian Day Conversion

Birth times are converted to **Julian Day numbers** ‚Äî a continuous count of days used in astronomy since 4713 BC. This ensures:
- Timezone handling is correct
- Calculations work for any date in history
- Precision down to the second

**Critical detail:** Local birth times are converted to UTC before calculations. Example: A birth at 2:00 PM in New York (EDT) is calculated as 6:00 PM UTC.

### The Four Angular Lines (MC, IC, AC, DC)

For each of the 10 planets, the app calculates 4 "angular" lines ‚Äî where the planet is at a key position relative to any location on Earth:

| Line | Full Name | Meaning | How It's Calculated |
|------|-----------|---------|---------------------|
| **MC** | Midheaven | Career, public image, highest achievement | Where the planet reaches its highest point in the sky (culmination) |
| **IC** | Imum Coeli | Home, roots, private life | Opposite of MC ‚Äî where the planet is at its lowest point |
| **AC** | Ascendant | Personal energy, identity | Where the planet rises on the eastern horizon |
| **DC** | Descendant | Relationships, partnerships | Where the planet sets on the western horizon |

### City Scoring Algorithm (Power Places)

The app matches 338 global cities against the user's 40 planetary lines using this formula:

```
Score = ProximityScore √ó LineTypeMultiplier √ó PlanetRelevance + MultiLineBonus
```

**Proximity scoring:**
- < 30 km from line ‚Üí 100 points (exceptional)
- < 75 km ‚Üí 90 points
- < 150 km ‚Üí 70 points
- < 250 km ‚Üí 50 points
- < 350 km ‚Üí 30 points
- 350-400 km ‚Üí 20 points

**Line type multipliers:**
- MC: 1.25√ó (most powerful)
- AC: 1.20√ó
- DC: 1.15√ó
- IC: 1.10√ó

**Planet relevance by category:**
- **Love:** Venus and Moon get 1.5√ó weight
- **Career:** Sun and Jupiter get 1.5√ó weight
- **Growth:** Jupiter and Uranus get 1.5√ó weight
- **Home:** Moon and Saturn get 1.5√ó weight

**Multi-line bonus:** +15 points if a city is near 2+ beneficial lines

### Power Months Calculation (2026 Forecast)

The app calculates which months are best for each life category by analyzing **planetary transits** ‚Äî the relationship between where planets are NOW (or in 2026) versus where they were at the user's birth.

**Aspect types scored:**

| Aspect | Angle | Nature | Power |
|--------|-------|--------|-------|
| Conjunction | 0¬∞ | Powerful alignment | 90% |
| Trine | 120¬∞ | Natural flow | 100% |
| Sextile | 60¬∞ | Opportunity | 100% |
| Square | 90¬∞ | Tension/action | 50% |
| Opposition | 180¬∞ | Awareness/balance | 70% |

**Daily sampling:** The app calculates aspects for EVERY DAY of a month, not just once. Tighter orbs (more exact aspects) score exponentially higher. This creates real differentiation between months.

**Normalization:** Raw scores are normalized to a 15-95 range within each category. Even "quiet" months don't show as 0 ‚Äî they're 15+, because there's always something happening.

**Result:** Each month gets a score (0-100) for each category:
- 80+ = "Power month" ‚Äî exceptional for that goal
- 60-79 = "Very strong"
- 40-59 = "Good"
- 20-39 = "Moderate"
- Below 20 = "Quiet" ‚Äî better for rest

### Confidence Levels (Unknown Birth Time)

If users don't know their birth time, the app:
1. Assumes noon as a midpoint
2. Runs calculations across multiple sampled times
3. Shows confidence levels based on how consistent results are

**Confidence labels:**
- **High:** Results consistent across time variations
- **Medium:** Some variation based on birth time
- **Low:** Results vary significantly

This is shown to users so they understand when predictions might be less reliable.

### Big Three Calculation

The "Big Three" (Sun, Moon, Rising) is calculated from:

**Sun sign:** Which zodiac constellation the Sun was in at birth (based on ecliptic longitude)

**Moon sign:** Which zodiac constellation the Moon was in at birth

**Rising sign (Ascendant):** Which zodiac constellation was rising on the eastern horizon at the moment of birth at the birth location

Each sign also has an associated **element** (Fire, Earth, Air, Water) which affects the dashboard's color theming.
